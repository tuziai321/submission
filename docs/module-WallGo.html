WallGo

Original two-player Wall-Go game logic.

The game uses a 7 by 7 board. Red and Blue each control four pieces. Two pieces per player begin in fixed starting positions, then the remaining pieces are placed in the order Red, Blue, Blue, Red. During normal play a player moves one piece up to two orthogonal steps, including zero steps, then builds one permanent wall beside that moved piece.
